CLASSES

The ballet class Raquel has developed is geared for professional dancers, incorporating an emphasis on musicality and finding new freedom and pathways of movement. It encourages dancers to use their own imagination through liberation of port de bras and dynamics through different qualities of movement, learning how to play with how the body moves energy through space. It is ideal for professional dancers looking to discover new ways of expanding the body's capacity by breaking through long-formed habits, while having a focus on the pure enjoyment of dance itself.
