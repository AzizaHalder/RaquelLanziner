ABOUT

Raquel is a Canadian born, dance artist based in Berlin. Born and raised in North Vancouver, Raquel Lanziner grew up in a musical household surrounded by singing and performing before she could speak. Before she began her formal dance training at the age of nine, her childhood was immersed in theater productions, piano recitals and musical theater.

At eighteen she finished the ballet program at Proarte in North Vancouver and proceeded to further her formation at Ecole Supérieure de Ballet du Québec (ESBQ) in Montreal. During her studies, she was a part of the student company, Le Jeune Ballet du Québec, where she had the opportunity to perform in many pieces, as well as tour to France and Mexico. Following her graduation from ESBQ Raquel was a part of the Professional Dance Program in Banff 2012.

She worked as a free-lance dancer in Montreal before moving to Berlin in 2016, extending her career to work for multiple independent choreographers and various collaborative projects. Raquel has had the privilege of performing pieces by Aszure Barton, Shawn Hounsell, Annabelle Lopez Ochoa, Andrea Peña, Helene Blackburn, Thierry Malandin, Mario Radacovsky, Ron de Jesus, Sharon Eyal, as well as classical works by Petipa and Balanchine.
